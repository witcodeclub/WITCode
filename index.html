About Us

WITCode was established on 28th October with the vision of empowering and inspiring women in technology. As a coding club based in a girls' college, WITCode is dedicated to fostering a vibrant and inclusive environment where students can learn, collaborate, and grow together in the field of technology.

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to explore programming, problem-solving, and innovation, regardless of their prior experience. Through workshops, coding competitions, hackathons, and mentorship programs, WITCode equips its members with the technical and soft skills needed to thrive in the tech industry.

At WITCode, we believe in learning by doing. Our members work on real-world projects, contribute to open-source communities, and stay updated with the latest trends in technology. We also collaborate with industry professionals and alumni to bridge the gap between academics and the professional world.

Whether you're a coding beginner or a tech enthusiast, WITCode welcomes you to join our community of like-minded individuals who are passionate about technology and its power to make a difference.

Our Mission

To inspire, educate, and empower women in technology by providing a supportive platform for learning, collaboration, and personal growth. We aim to foster creativity, leadership, and confidence through innovative activities and real-world tech exposure.

Our Vision

To create a future where women lead the way in technology, breaking barriers and redefining the tech landscape. WITCode envisions a world where diversity drives innovation and every woman is equipped to contribute to and shape the digital age.
